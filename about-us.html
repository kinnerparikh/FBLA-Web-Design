Who We Are

Infiniti is a budget airline serving the West Coast of the United States. We offer flights in 9 major cities, stretching from Seattle to San Diego. Whether you are planning for a vacation nearby, visiting relatives from another state, or just looking to get away and explore somewhere new, Infiniti is always there and ready to bring you wherever you need to be.

Sustainability

The environment is becoming a major concern in relation to the aviation industry, and we make sure to do our part in order to lead a more sustainable world.
